# How to present this project honestly in an application

## Strong formulation

> I built ResilientCore Lab to deepen my understanding of carrier-network architecture and the operational disciplines behind critical infrastructure. The lab models a redundant PE/P/CE topology, IS-IS underlay, MPLS/Segment Routing concepts, MP-BGP L3VPN segmentation, Ansible-based validation, change/rollback planning and third-line troubleshooting workflows.

## Important boundary

Do not claim:

- 7 years of operator-network experience
- production Cisco/MPLS responsibility you have not had
- CCNP/CCNA if you do not hold the certification
- security-cleared critical-infrastructure experience if you do not have it

Instead say:

> My professional background is not yet at the senior networking level described in the role. I built this lab specifically to demonstrate how I learn, structure technical problems and translate theory into a documented, testable environment. I am applying because critical infrastructure and secure communications are areas I genuinely want to grow into.
