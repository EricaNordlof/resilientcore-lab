# Cost and schedule estimate template

A network solution is not complete until technical design, implementation effort and operational impact are understood.

| Item | Quantity | Unit estimate | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Router/interface capacity | 2 | TBD | Validate optics, licenses and slot capacity |
| Optics/transceivers | 4 | TBD | Match distance and fiber type |
| Engineering design | 16 h | TBD | HLD/LLD, addressing, routing policy |
| Lab/validation | 8 h | TBD | Test failure and rollback paths |
| Implementation | 8 h | TBD | Maintenance window |
| Documentation | 4 h | TBD | As-built + runbook updates |

## Schedule inputs

- procurement lead time
- change approval lead time
- customer coordination
- maintenance window availability
- lab/testing time
- rollback contingency

## Commercial disclaimer

This template demonstrates engineering planning only. Real quotations require current vendor pricing, license terms, capacity data and approved commercial assumptions.
